Welcome to Le Cambodge Restaurant

The Best Place You Can Enjoy the Best Food

Le Cambodge Restaurant ocated in the Phnom Penh City. The restaurant overlooks one of the most beautiful in Phnom Penh. Located here since 2000, it just underwent a full-scale renovation and redesign.The restaurant has been owned by Noch Channika and Restaurateur Will Guidara since 2011 and during that time it has evolved considerably, both in food and in experience. Today, the restaurant offers an eight to ten course menu in the main dining room, but guests can also visit the bar for a more abbreviated tasting menu, some light snacks, coffee or simply for a cocktail or glass of wine.
